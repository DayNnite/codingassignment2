Grilled Teriyaki Chicken

This recipe is from "The Recipe Critic" published by Alyssa Rivers: Grilled Teriyaki Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Optional: sesame seeds and sliced green onions for garnish

Equipment

  • Grill or grill pan
  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • Measuring cups and spoons
  • Sharp knife
  • Cutting board

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, minced garlic, ground ginger, and honey. Whisk until the sugar is dissolved and the ingredients are well combined.
  2. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish. Pour half of the teriyaki marinade over the chicken, ensuring they are well coated. Seal the bag or cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to 2 hours for better flavor.
  3. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the used marinade.
  4. Grill the chicken thighs for about 6–7 minutes per side, or until they reach an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and have nice grill marks.
  5. While the chicken is grilling, prepare the teriyaki sauce. In a small saucepan, combine the remaining teriyaki marinade with cornstarch and water. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens. Remove from heat.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes. Slice the chicken into strips if desired.
  7. Serve the grilled teriyaki chicken with the thickened teriyaki sauce drizzled over the top. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions if desired. Enjoy!
